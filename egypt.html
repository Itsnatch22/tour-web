Pyramids Of Giza

The **Pyramids of Giza** stand as a testament to the grandeur and mystery of ancient Egypt. Located near **Cairo**, these pyramids include the **Great Pyramid of Khufu**, the **Pyramid of Khafre**, and the **Pyramid of Menkaure**. The **Sphinx**, a giant limestone statue, guards these majestic structures. Visiting the pyramids offers a glimpse into the rich history of the ancient Egyptians.

  • Highlights: Great Pyramid of Khufu, Sphinx, Solar Boat Museum
  • Best time to visit: October to April
  • Recommended Duration: 1-2 Days
