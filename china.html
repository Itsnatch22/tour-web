The Great Wall Of China

The Great Wall of China is an ancient series of walls and fortifications, totaling over 13,000 miles, originally built to protect Chinese states and empires from invaders. It stretches from **Liaoning Province** to **Gansu Province**, winding through mountains, deserts, and plains. Tourists often visit the **Mutianyu** and **Badaling sections**, which are known for their beautiful scenery and easy access.

  • Highlights: Badaling, Mutianyu, Jinshanling
  • Best time to visit: Spring and Autumn (March-May, September-November)
  • Recommended Duration: 1-2 Days
